simple

but not simplistic

Bare is a collection of boutique stays nestled in the hidden nooks of Saigons storied old apartments. Just a brisk walk away from downtown, our rooms are the perfect home base to begin your exploration of the citys rich cultural, historical, and most importantly, culinary heritage.

Bare embodies a grounded, unabashed sense of self. We believe in staying true to our nature in every situation, so we created Bare in the hope of inspiring you to do the same during your stay. Eat fresh lychees on the floor, soak in Saigons summer drizzle, or even lounge around the room with no pants come bare with us, be yourself.

Take in the breathtaking view of Saigon at night from the balcony, bask in the morning sun along the breezy canal or simply stay in bed to relish a cup of Vietnamese c-ph-. Enjoy the full local experience with Bares attentive service, personalized amenities and sleek architectural design.
